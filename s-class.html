The technology highlights of the S-Class

Active Lane Keeping Assist

If you inadvertently cross a broken or continuous lane marking, the system can warn you by making the steering wheel vibrate. in the case of a continuous line, or if the lane on the other side of a broken line has been identified as occupied, the assistance system can intervene further with one-sided braking.

Driving Assistance package

Safely takes you to your destination feeling relaxed: thanks to the modern, intelligently networked sensor systems, you can enjoy the many benefits of conditionally automated driving. Numerous assistance systems support you as the situation requires, warning you if there is a risk of collision and intervening if necessary for the benefit of all road users.

AIRMATIC

AIRMATIC combines the advantages of several suspension systems in one choose between comfort and dynamism. The set-up range is particularly impressive.
