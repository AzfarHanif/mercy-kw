Technology

The technology highlights of the GLS.

Active Lane Keeping Assist

If you inadvertently cross a broken or continuous lane marking, the system can warn you by making the steering wheel vibrate. in the case of a continuous line, or if the lane on the other side of a broken line has been identified as occupied, the assistance system can intervene further with one-sided braking.

Active Brake Assist

Active Brake Assist can help you to avoid rear-ending moving or stationary vehicles ahead, as well as prevent accidents with moving pedestrians or cyciists. As part ot this, in addition to distance and collision warning, the system primarily also offers situation- related braking assistance - right up to autonomous emergency braking.

4MATIC all-wheel drive

With its 4MATIC all-wheel drive and the special off-road drive program, [g]the GLS is able to master both light and heavy terrain. Additional help is provided by the Torque on Demand (Singie Speed) longitudinal transfer case which can increase traction on meadows, gravel and light terrain.
